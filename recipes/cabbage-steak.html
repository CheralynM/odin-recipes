Cabbage Steak

This is a savory cabbage side dish. This recipe is very easy to make and takes very little time to prepare.

Ingredients

  • 1 Head of Cabbage
  • 1/4 Cup Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Garlic Powder
  • Paprika
  • Red Pepper Flakes *Optional*

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
  2. Wash the head of cabbage and dry it with a towel. Then, Cut the stems off of the cabbage. Next, cut the cabbage in half. Finally, cut each half in half again. Each section should be 3/4-inch to 1-inch thick
  3. Take your baking sheet and line it with parchment paper. Next, place the cabbage sections on the parchment paper and allow some space around each one.
  4. Brush each cabbage section with olive oil until they are all coated thoroughly.Next, sprinkle each section with salt, garlic powder, and paprika.
  5. Flip the cabbage sections over and repeat Step 4. Add a pinch of red pepper flakes to each one if desired.
  6. Put the cabbage sections into the oven at 400 degrees for about 25 minutes, until the leaves are browned and the center is tender. Serve hot out of the oven.
